Single Beam Crane

LD model single beam crane is characterized by more reasonable structure and higher strength steel as a whole. Used together with electric hoist as a complete set, it is light duty crane with a capacity of 1 to 5 tons. The span is 7.5m-31.5m. Working grade is A3-A5. Working temperature is -25℃to 40℃.

It is widely used in plants, warehouse, material stocks to lift goods. It is prohibited to use the equipment in the combustible, explosive or corrosive environment. The main operating method is ground control(ground control, remote control and cabin control). If the span and lifting height is out of the range that parameter lists, we can do non-standard design according to user requirement.

Main features:

  1. Compact structure, good rigidity and sensitive operation
  2. Energy saving and lower maintenance cost
  3. Soft-start motor, lower the impact force
  4. Low building height
  5. Monocoque trolley and nice appearance
