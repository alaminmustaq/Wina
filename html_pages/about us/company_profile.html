Company Profile

Henan Kuangshan Crane Co.,Ltd. is mainly engaged in the design, manufacture and sales of "Mining Source" brand bridges, gantry cranes, electric hoists and their accessories. It is the vice chairman unit of China Heavy Machinery Industry Association and a member unit of China Quality Inspection Association. The construction area is over 680,000 square meters.

Henan Mine Intelligent Industrial Park, which started construction in March 2019, has a construction area of ​​740,000 square meters and a total investment of 2.6 billion yuan in a five-year construction period. It mainly includes 6 standardized crane production workshops, a comprehensive office building main building and living supporting facilities.

The company currently has a total construction area of ​​1.42 million square meters, more than 3,500 employees, more than 428 sales and service organizations at home and abroad, more than 2,000 sets of various processing equipment, and can independently complete more than 20 technological processes such as turning, planing, and grinding. It has advanced technology research and development institutions and a team of senior experts at home and abroad. With an annual output of more than 80,000 sets of single and double beam cranes.

Products are sold to all parts of the country and more than 80 countries and regions, and enter the "One Belt One Road" international market. Products are used in machinery, metallurgy, mining, electric power, railway, aerospace, port, petroleum, chemical and other industries.

The company has successively won the national "Contract-honoring and Credit-Reliable Enterprise", "High-tech Enterprise", "National Industrial Enterprise Quality Benchmark", "National Green Factory", "National Intellectual Property Advantage Enterprise", and "National Standardization Pilot for Integration of Industrialization and Industrialization" There are more than 300 provincial and provincial honors such as "Model Enterprise", "National Model Workers' Home", "Quality Award of the Governor of Henan Province", "Intelligent Factory of Henan Province", and "Top 100 Enterprises in Henan Province".
